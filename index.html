Services

Conceptual Design

We are the experts at creating a variety of creative mechanical solutions for complex needs. It begins with the creation of a ´Technical Specification´ to clearly outline all of the needs of the proposed system. We then use our extensive design knowledge, combined with appropiate research, to create a variety of well annotated solutions. invisible text

Detailed Design

We have extensive experience in transitioning from concept to physical reality. The process typically begins with an evaluation of possible concepts, following which, the chosen concept is modelled on our in-house CAD software, analysed for structural stability; and prototyped a number of times until we are happy to release the final drawings to production.

CAD & Drafting Support

Do you need a helping hand with CAD and/or drafting for your project? We utilise our extensive CAD and drafting skillset to provide both in-house and remote support, tailored to your specific needs. Support can be provided at any point in product lifecycle and for any duration, where any type of CAD software can be accomodated with ease.

Case Studies

Exroid Technology Limited

We recently completed a project with Exroid Technology - the pioneers of a non-invasive treatment for haemorrhoids. They approached us with the desire to upgrade their existing device to a next generation model. We worked with them to clarify the needs of the new device, creating a clear technical specification, prior to the conceptualisation of multiple design options. The chosen design incorporated numerous improvements, including improved ease of assembly and a lower manufacturing cost.

Autoguide Technology

Autoguide were looking to create a novel hydrallically powered machine, that would be capbable of inserting screw piles inot the ground without the need for manual intervention. We began by capturing the key requirements and needs of the device, prior to the conceptualisation of a variety of design solutions for each function of the machine's operation. The resulting design solution for the entire machine was presented from a design and business perpective, which Autoguide were happy to move forward to the deatiled design phase and then onto production.

Volunteer West Africa

Volunteer West Africa were looking to significantly improve their facilities with a new medical centre and education building. We were part of the team that undertook an in-depth study, to ascertain the feasibility of the new compound. This began with numerous conversations with members of the local community, to clearly understand the needs of the new compound. We then created the technical specification, conceptualised many design options, performed appropiate design and costing calculations; and finally modelled the most promising solutions on Google Sketchup.
